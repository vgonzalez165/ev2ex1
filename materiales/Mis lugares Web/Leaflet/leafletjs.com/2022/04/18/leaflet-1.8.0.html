Leaflet 1.8 released in the middle of war

Posted on 18 April 2022 by Volodymyr Agafonkin

We have just released Leaflet v1.8.0, a culmination of 1.5 years of development. This is a huge release focused on bug fixes, major reliability and accessibility improvements, cleaning up legacy code, and numerous improvements to documentation, development workflow and release process.
